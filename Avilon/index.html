About Us

The Edo Future500 (EDF500) fellowship program is an initiative of the Edo State Skills Development Agency (EdoJobs), set up to build the next generation of industry experts in Edo State from the key sectors in Nigeria. The fellowship will bring together business owners and entrepreneurs in Edo State and provide them with the right knowledge ,skills and resources towards the building and development of sustainable businesses for the economic growth and development of Edo State.

ABOUT THE FELLOWSHIP

Fellowship Overview

Edo Future500 (EDF500) is an initiative of the Edo State Skills Development Agency, to build the next generation of industry experts in key sectors in Nigeria.



The goal of the program is to provide young entrepreneurs with the right knowledge, depth, commitment and experience to become industry experts within 5 years. The process would bring together 500 budding entrepreneurs from all LGAs in Edo State and set them on a path to thrive sustainably

Fellowship Components

  • Sales Support and Market Expansion

    • During the fellowship, fellows will participate in activites to boost business sales and expand their market reach.

  • Professional Skills Development

    • During the fellowship, fellows will be provided with professional skills development training based on their area of interest.

  • Improve Business Visibility

    • Fellows will be provided with opportunities to increase the visibility of their businesses beyond their business locality or region.

  • Attachment to Mentors

    • During the fellowship, fellows will be attached to mentors to provide them with guidance and support towards making the right decisions for business growth.

  • Business Formalization

    • During the fellowship, fellows whose businesses have not been formalized will be provided with business formalization.

  • Access to International Markets

    • During the fellowship, fellows will be provided with resources and opportunities to access the international market.

  • Access to Finance

    • At the end of the fellowship program, fellows will be provided with funding opportunities to boost their business.

Application and Selection Process

Selection Process and Criteria

The Edo Future500 fellowship selection process is a merit-based open competition. After the deadline, all eligible applicants will be reviewed by independent business organizations. Following this review, chosen applicants will be interviewed. Selected applicants will be inducted to participate in the fellowship program.

Who is eligible to apply?

Applicants wil not be discriminated against on the basis of socio-economic status, disability, educational background, religion or state of origin. The Edo Future500 Fellowship is open to entrepreneurs and business owners who meets the following criteria:


  • All applicants must be resident in Edo State with an operational business, minimum 2 years.

  • Are between the ages of 18 and 40 on or before the application deadline.

  • Are willing to complete all necessary coursework and trainings.

  • Are proficient in reading, writing and speaking English.

  • Applicants must have a proven track record of participating in either leadership or community development activities.

  • Applicants must have a keen interest in the development of Edo State.

  • Applicants must have a proven record of accomplishment in their selected field of business

  • Applicants must have great social and communication skills which will include writing and public speaking.

Please note that in the event of any discrepancy or if information provided is found to be false, the application will immediately be declared invalid and the applicant ineligible.

What are the criteria for selection?

The following criteria will be used to evaluate applications (not in order of importance):

  • A proven record of leadership and accomplishment in business or any related field;
  • A demonstrated commitment to community development or public service;
  • The ability to work cooperatively in diverse groups and to respect the opinions of others;
  • Strong social and communication skills;
  • An energetic, positive attitude;
  • A demonstrated knowledge of, interest in, and professional experience in the preferred business sector; and

  • A commitment to implement and apply all skills and trainings to the development of Edo State.

ABOUT EDOJOBS BUSINESS COMMUNITY

Details about EDOJOBS business community

The EdoJobs business community is an initiative of Edo State Skills Development Agency (EdoJobs), set up to empower entrepreneurs and business owners residing in Edo State with opportunities to grow their businesses. With this community, we intend to equip business owners with the right knowledge and resources, foster collaboration and challenge business owners towards maximizing opportunities for growth. The community is open with no criteria, to all business owners and entrepreneurs resident in Edo State.
